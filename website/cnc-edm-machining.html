Mold Machining

Complete Mold Manufacturing Capability

Every process needed to build a production-grade injection mold — from raw steel to polished, tested tooling — done under one roof.

CNC Milling & Turning

High-speed CNC machining for mold bases, cores, cavities, and structural components. Makino machining centers on-site. Tight tolerances, smooth surfaces.

  • → Mold plates and bases
  • → Core and cavity blocks
  • → Metal structural parts
  • → Tolerances to ±0.01mm

EDM — Sinker EDM

Electrical discharge machining for complex cavities, deep ribs, and sharp internal corners that CNC cannot reach. Essential for high-precision injection mold cavities.

  • → Complex cavity geometry
  • → Deep ribs and slots
  • → Hardened steel machining
  • → Mirror-level surface finish

WEDM — Wire-Cut EDM

Wire electrical discharge machining for precision cutting of complex 2D and 3D profiles in hardened tool steel. Used for punches, inserts, and intricate mold components.

  • → Precision profile cutting
  • → Hardened steel up to 65 HRC
  • → Tapered and complex cuts
  • → Tight dimensional accuracy
