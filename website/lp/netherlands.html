FAQ

Netherlands Buyers: Common Questions

What import duties apply when importing mould inserts from China to the Netherlands?

EU MFN duty on injection mould components (HS 8480.71) from China is 1.7–2.7%. Dutch BTW (21%) is applied on the customs value plus duty. If you are BTW-registered, the full 21% BTW is recoverable as voorbelasting (input tax) on your quarterly BTW return — so your effective import cost is just 1.7–2.7% duty. We provide a full commercial invoice and CN22/CN23 export documents. Under DDP terms, we pre-pay all charges at Rotterdam and deliver to your door with zero customs paperwork.

Why does delivery go through Rotterdam, and how does that benefit Dutch buyers?

Rotterdam is the largest port in Europe and has the most developed customs clearance infrastructure on the continent. DHL Express shipments from Ningbo entering the EU via Rotterdam benefit from same-day customs processing — significantly faster than smaller EU ports. After Rotterdam clearance, domestic NL delivery takes just 1 working day. This gives Netherlands-based buyers a total door-to-door time of 6–8 days from Ningbo — faster than almost any other EU destination.

Do you accept SEPA bank transfer and quote in EUR?

Yes. EUR is our default quote currency for Dutch buyers, and SEPA bank transfer (IBAN) is our preferred payment method at no additional cost. Wise (TransferWise) and PayPal are also accepted. The EUR rate is fixed at order confirmation so there are no currency surprises. A full commercial invoice in English is provided for your Dutch BTW voorbelasting reclaim.

Is your documentation compatible with ASML or Philips supply chain requirements?

Yes. We provide full material traceability certificates (powder lot to finished part), first article inspection reports (FAIR) with CMM dimensional data, process qualification records, and pressure test certificates. For semiconductor equipment enclosure supply chains (ASML-tier, NXP, Océ/Canon), we can provide ISO 9001-aligned quality records and support cleanroom-compatible packaging requirements. Contact us with your specific supplier qualification requirements and we'll confirm coverage.

What documentation do you provide for Dutch medical device customers (Abbott, Zimmer, Boston Scientific)?

For medical device applications, we supply: material certificates with full traceability to powder lot, first article inspection report (FAIR) with CMM data, HIP treatment certificate (if applicable), process qualification documentation, and can support ISO 13485 quality records. For EU MDR and IVD technical file requirements, we can provide supporting documentation. Contact us to discuss your specific regulatory documentation needs before ordering.

Can you serve other EU countries from the Netherlands order?

Yes. We serve the full EU market. Rotterdam's position as Europe's main port makes it the natural EU entry point for shipments to Netherlands, Belgium, Germany, France, and beyond. DDP delivery is available across EU. Lead times to most major EU cities are 8–12 days total door-to-door. Contact us with your delivery country and we'll confirm transit times and DDP pricing.
