Every conformal cooling insert is hydrostatic pressure-tested at 200 bar (20 MPa) before shipment. Leak-free certification issued with test date, test pressure, hold time, and pass result. No insert leaves our facility without this test — eliminating cooling circuit failures in production.

FAQ

German Buyers: Häufig gestellte Fragen

What EU import duties apply when importing mould inserts from China to Germany?

EU MFN duty on injection mould components (HS 8480.71) from China is 1.7–2.7%. German Mehrwertsteuer (19% VAT) is applied on the customs value (CIF) plus duty at the German border. The 19% import VAT is fully recoverable as Vorsteuer (input tax deduction) on your Umsatzsteuervoranmeldung if you are a VAT-registered business — making the effective cost only the 1.7–2.7% duty. With DDP shipping, MouldNova's freight partner handles Zollabfertigung and pre-pays all charges. You receive the package with zero customs paperwork.

How long does DHL Express shipping take from Ningbo, China to Germany?

DHL Express transit from Ningbo to Munich, Stuttgart, Frankfurt, Düsseldorf, and Cologne: typically 5–7 working days. Hamburg, Berlin, and Dresden: 6–8 working days. Manufacturing (LPBF + post-processing + inspection + documentation): 5–8 business days. Total DDP lead time from order confirmation: 10–15 working days. Expedited manufacturing (3–5 days) available for urgent tooling requirements — contact us to discuss.

Are your inserts compatible with BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Bosch, or VW supplier quality requirements?

Yes. We manufacture to IT6/IT7 tolerances per DIN ISO 286 on mating faces and provide a full FAIR (First Article Inspection Report) with CMM-measured dimensional data. Material certificates with full powder-lot traceability, hydrostatic pressure test certificates (200 bar), and process records support PPAP/APQP submissions. We can discuss specific VDA 6.1, IATF 16949, or customer-specific quality plan requirements. Contact us to review your customer's supplier quality requirements document (SQR).

Can you provide ISO 13485 documentation for medical device mould applications?

Yes. For medical device mould applications serving Siemens Healthineers, Karl Storz, Braun Melsungen or other medical device OEMs, we provide: full material certificate with powder-lot traceability, FAIR with CMM data, hydrostatic pressure test certificate, HIP treatment certificate (where applicable), and ISO 13485-compatible quality records. For CE marking support or IVD regulation documentation, contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Commercial invoice in English with your purchase order reference number is provided for every shipment. Contact us to discuss payment terms for repeat orders.

What materials do you use and what properties do they achieve?

We use two primary LPBF materials for conformal cooling inserts: (1) 420 Stainless Steel — hardness HRC 48–52 after heat treatment, good corrosion resistance, cost-effective for most injection mold applications; (2) 18Ni300 Maraging Steel — hardness HRC 52–56, excellent toughness, preferred for high-cycle automotive tooling and glass-filled or abrasive polymers. Both achieve density >99.5% by default, >99.9% with optional HIP treatment. Full mechanical property data sheets available on request.
