The Market Shift

From Conventional Drilled Cooling to LPBF Conformal Channels

Italian mold shops that still rely on conventional straight-drilled cooling circuits are leaving significant cycle time and quality gains on the table. LPBF (Laser Powder Bed Fusion) conformal cooling channels follow the part geometry at a uniform offset — achieving temperature uniformity that straight channels physically cannot reach on complex surfaces. Early adopters in the Bologna packaging sector and Turin automotive cluster are reporting 25–40% cycle reductions and significant reductions in warpage-related rejects.

Italian Buyers: Domande Frequenti

What EU import duties apply when importing mould inserts from China to Italy?

EU MFN duty on injection mould components (HS 8480.71) from China is 1.7–2.7%. Italian IVA (Imposta sul Valore Aggiunto) at 22% is applied on the customs value (CIF) plus duty at the Italian border. The 22% import IVA is fully recoverable as detrazione IVA (input tax credit) on your periodic IVA return for businesses registered with a valid partita IVA — making the effective additional cost only the 1.7–2.7% EU duty. With DDP shipping, MouldNova's freight partner handles all Italian customs formalities and pre-pays all charges. You receive the package with zero customs paperwork.
