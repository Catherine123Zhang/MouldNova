Canada–China Import Summary

0% MFN Duty for Mold Components — Canada's Best Import Rate

Canada's MFN tariff on injection mold components (HS 8480.71) is 0% — significantly better than the US (3.1% MFN + 25% Section 301). This makes Canada one of the most cost-effective destinations for Chinese-origin conformal cooling inserts.
