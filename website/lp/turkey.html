Import & Customs — Turkey

Turkish Import Duties & KDV (Katma Değer Vergisi) — Clear Summary

Turkey applies MFN tariff rates on imports from China. The 20% KDV (Turkish VAT) on imports is fully recoverable by VAT-registered businesses — your real cost is only the MFN duty.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — Turkey

What import duties apply when importing mould inserts from China to Turkey?

Turkey applies MFN (Most Favoured Nation / WTO) tariff rates on imports from China. Turkey has a Customs Union with the EU for industrial goods, but this does not extend to Chinese-origin goods — China is not part of that union, so EU rates do not apply to China imports. For injection mould tooling inserts (HS 8480.71), the Turkish MFN duty is typically 0–5% for precision tooling components. In addition, 20% Turkish KDV (Katma Değer Vergisi) is applied on the customs value (CIF value plus duty). The 20% KDV is fully recoverable by KDV-registered businesses — your real net cost is only the 0–5% MFN duty. We strongly recommend confirming the exact HS code with your gümrük müşaviri (customs broker) before ordering.

What materials do you use and what properties do they achieve?

We use two primary LPBF materials: (1) 420 Stainless Steel — hardness HRC 48–52 after heat treatment, good corrosion resistance, cost-effective for most injection mold applications including white goods and packaging; (2) 18Ni300 Maraging Steel — hardness HRC 52–56, excellent toughness, preferred for high-cycle automotive tooling and glass-filled or abrasive polymers (PA66-GF30, POM, ABS).
