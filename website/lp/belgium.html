FAQ

Belgian & European Buyers: Common Questions

What EU import duties apply when importing mold inserts from China?

EU MFN duty on injection mold components (HS 8480.71) is typically 1.7–2.7%. Belgian VAT (21%) is applied on the customs value plus duty. However, the Belgian VAT is fully recoverable if you are a VAT-registered business (BTW/TVA number). We provide a full commercial invoice and CN22/CN23 export documents to facilitate customs clearance. DDP shipping option means we pre-pay all charges and deliver to your door with no surprise costs.

Can you provide documentation compatible with EU medical device regulations (MDR)?

Yes. For medical device mold applications, we provide: material certificates with full traceability to powder lot, first article inspection report (FAIR) with CMM data, process qualification documentation, HIP treatment certificate (if applicable), and can support ISO 13485 quality records. For CE marking support, we can provide technical file documentation. Contact us to discuss your specific MDR/IVD requirements.

What are the realistic lead times to Belgium?

Manufacturing (LPBF + post-processing + inspection): 5–8 business days. DHL Express Ningbo to Belgium (Antwerp, Brussels, Ghent): 3–4 days. Total DDP delivery: 8–12 days from order confirmation. Expedited options available for urgent orders. Belgium's Liege Airport (Cargo hub) and Antwerp port are both well-served by DHL Express from China.

Do you quote in EUR?

Yes. We quote in EUR or USD depending on your preference. Exchange rate is fixed at the time of order confirmation. Wire transfers (SWIFT), PayPal, and Wise (TransferWise) are accepted for EUR payments. VAT invoice in English provided for your accounting.

Can you serve other EU countries beyond Belgium?

Absolutely. We serve the full EU market including Netherlands (Rotterdam), Germany (Munich, Stuttgart), France, Italy, Poland, and others. DDP shipping applies across EU. Lead times to most EU cities: 8–12 days. Contact us with your country and we'll confirm transit times.
