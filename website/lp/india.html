Questions from Indian Customers

Common Questions

What is the import duty on mold inserts from China to India?

Mold inserts (HS code 8480.71 or 8480.79) typically attract 7.5–10% Basic Customs Duty plus GST. We provide a commercial invoice with accurate HS code and CIF value to make customs clearance straightforward. Most of our Indian customers handle customs themselves or via a freight forwarder.

Can I do a small trial order first?

Absolutely. We accept single-piece orders. Many Indian customers start with 1–2 inserts to test fit, finish, and performance in their mold before placing larger repeat orders. There's no surcharge for small quantities.

What payment methods do you accept?

T/T (bank wire transfer) — 50% advance to start production, 50% balance before shipping. We provide full banking details and a pro-forma invoice. PayPal available for smaller orders. We do not use agents or middlemen, so 100% of your payment comes to us directly.

How do you handle quality issues or wrong dimensions?

We ship with a CMM dimensional inspection report. If any dimension does not match your drawing, we remake the insert at no charge. We've shipped to India multiple times — we know how important getting it right first time is when shipping costs and lead times are involved.

Is conformal cooling suitable for Indian plastics like PP, ABS, and Nylon?

Yes. Conformal cooling works with all thermoplastics. It's especially impactful for PP (large, complex parts), ABS (cosmetic surfaces, burn marks), Nylon (hygroscopic materials that need precise thermal management), and any transparent material (PETG, PS). If you're unsure, share your part and we'll run a free thermal simulation to show the expected improvement.
