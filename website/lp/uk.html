UK Import Cost Summary

UK MFN Duty: 1.7–2.7% — VAT Fully Recoverable

The UK Global Tariff applies a low MFN duty of approximately 1.7–2.7% on mould tooling inserts from China — a modest cost on a high-value precision component.

20% UK VAT is charged at the border on the customs value (CIF + duty). For VAT-registered businesses this is fully recoverable as input tax on the next VAT return — making the effective VAT cost zero.

Postponed VAT Accounting (PVA) is available for UK VAT-registered importers, allowing you to account for import VAT on your VAT return rather than paying at the border — improving cash flow.

Net effective additional cost for VAT-registered businesses: 1.7–2.7% MFN duty only.

Why Conformal Cooling Delivers Such Fast ROI

Conventional straight-drilled cooling channels cannot follow the geometry of the mould cavity. Hot spots persist, forcing longer cooling times to prevent warpage. Conformal channels — printed at 0.4 mm proximity to the cavity wall — eliminate hot spots and reduce cooling time by 20–55%.

On a high-cavitation UK moulding line running 24/5 or 24/7, even a 3–4 second cycle reduction compounds rapidly. The insert cost is typically recovered within the first week of production restart.
