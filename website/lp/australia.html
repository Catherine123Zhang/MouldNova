The Problem

Conventional Cooling Is Costing You Production Time

Traditional straight-drilled cooling channels can't follow complex part geometry. The result: uneven mold temperatures, longer cycle times, warped parts, and higher scrap rates.

Conventional Cooling Problems

  • Hot spots near complex geometry → warpage and sink marks
  • Long cycle times (50–65% of cycle is cooling)
  • High scrap rates from non-uniform shrinkage
  • Limited by drill bit straight lines
  • Pressure drops from sharp 90° intersections

Conformal Cooling Solutions

  • Channels follow exact part contour — uniform cooling everywhere
  • 20–55% faster cycle times measured on production molds
  • Scrap reduction 15–40% from consistent part dimensions
  • Complex 3D channel paths only possible with LPBF metal 3D printing
  • Smooth curved channels minimize pressure drop

FAQ

Australian Buyers: Common Questions

Do I pay import duties when importing mold inserts from China to Australia?

Under ChAFTA (China–Australia Free Trade Agreement), most manufactured metal goods including mold components are subject to 0% import duty. Standard Australian GST (10%) applies on the customs value plus freight. DHL handles customs clearance — your invoice and Certificate of Origin (Form for ChAFTA) are provided by us. Confirm the HS code with your freight forwarder or customs broker for your specific parts.

What are the realistic lead times to Australian cities?

Manufacturing (LPBF print + post-processing + inspection): 7–10 business days. DHL Express Ningbo to Melbourne/Sydney: 3–4 days. Total: 10–14 days for east coast cities. Perth and regional locations may add 1–2 days. Expedited print services available for urgent projects (+30% cost).

What file formats do you accept for quotes?

We accept STEP (.stp/.step), IGES (.igs/.iges), Parasolid (.x_t), and native files from SolidWorks, CATIA, NX, and Creo. 2D drawings (PDF/DWG) are sufficient for initial quoting. If you don't have CAD, our engineers can model from 2D drawings or your sample part.

Do you have experience with Australian industry standards?

Yes. We work regularly with Australian automotive Tier 1 suppliers and can provide documentation compatible with IATF 16949 quality systems. For medical device customers, we provide full material traceability, inspection reports to AS/NZS ISO 9001 standards, and can supply FDA-compliant documentation where required.

What are your payment terms for Australian customers?

Standard: 50% deposit upon order confirmation, 50% balance before shipment. Payment via T/T (bank transfer in AUD or USD), PayPal, or Wise (formerly TransferWise). First-time orders: full payment upfront. Established customers: extended terms on request. We provide GST-exclusive invoices with all required fields for Australian business accounting.

Can you quote urgently for a mold that's already running but experiencing hot spots?

Yes — send us your part drawing, current insert dimensions, and thermal images or cycle data showing the hot spot location. We can turn around a quote within 24 hours and prioritise production for urgent retrofit projects. Expedited lead time: 7–10 days total (manufacturing + shipping).
