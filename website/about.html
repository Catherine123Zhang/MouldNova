About Saiguang 3D Technology

A high-tech manufacturer specializing in metal 3D printing, conformal cooling inserts, CNC machining, and vision inspection — serving injection molders and toolmakers worldwide.

Our Mission

Making the World Cooler Through 3D Printing

Ningbo Saiguang 3D Technology Co., Ltd. was founded with a single mission — to make the world cooler through 3D printing. We help injection molders, toolmakers, and product manufacturers achieve higher quality, faster cycles, and lower costs through conformal cooling technology and additive manufacturing.

Our facility is located in Mazhu Small Business Park, Yuyao City, Ningbo — one of China's most established manufacturing hubs. We operate three state-of-the-art metal 3D printing machines, including BLT and E-Plus systems, enabling us to take on projects from single prototypes to production batches.

"3D printing brings a qualitative leap in product quality for our customers — and makes the world a cooler place. We provide efficient, high-quality mold solutions."
