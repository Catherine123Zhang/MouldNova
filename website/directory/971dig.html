Hunan Dingli Science & Technology Co., Ltd.

湖南顶立科技股份有限公司

Company Overview

Founded in 2006, Hunan Dingli Science & Technology Co., Ltd. specializes in R&D, manufacturing, and sales of specialty materials and advanced thermal processing equipment. A controlling subsidiary of Chujian New Materials (A-share listed: 002171), it has developed the novel ultra-high-speed plasma rotating electrode process (N-PREP) powder-making system, producing ultra-pure high-temperature alloy powders. Its product range includes nickel-based superalloys, high-strength steels, cobalt-chromium-molybdenum alloys, tungsten-nickel-iron heavy alloys, high-entropy alloys, and titanium alloys—widely applied in aerospace, tooling, medical, and automotive sectors. Dingli has established a full metal 3D printing industrial chain covering master alloy design, melting, powder production, AM processing, heat treatment, and performance testing, offering comprehensive metal AM turnkey solutions.
