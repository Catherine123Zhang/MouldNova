Company Overview

Beijing Makera Technology Co., Ltd. (Makera) is a global leader in digital fabrication and intelligent manufacturing tools and services. It develops and manufactures desktop-scale intelligent digital fabrication equipment—including CNC engravers, 3D printers, and laser cutters—for makers, education/research institutions, and rapid prototyping in enterprises. Makera also pioneers new product lines in robotics and AI. Its flagship 'Ke Weilai™ Smart 3D Engraver' launched successfully on Kickstarter in 2021, raising over USD 1.7 million—the highest-funded CNC project on the platform.
