Shenzhen Shunyuan Optics Co., Ltd.

深圳市顺远光学有限责任公司

Company Overview

Founded in August 2022 and headquartered in Longhua District, Shenzhen, Shenzhen Shunyuan Optics Co., Ltd. evolved from the optics division of Shenzhen Wanshunxing Technology Co., Ltd. Since 2020, it has specialized in R&D, manufacturing, and sales of laser optics, holding multiple invention patents and independent IP rights. Shunyuan Optics operates under standardized, regulated management and was among the first to achieve ISO 9001:2015 certification. Its product range includes telecentric F-theta lenses, achromatic F-theta lenses, flat-field F-theta lenses, silicon carbide mirrors, high-power laser lenses, and fused silica cylindrical lenses. With over 120 R&D and production engineers and a dedicated service team, it delivers premium laser optical components and services worldwide.
