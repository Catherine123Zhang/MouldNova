Weifang Kaihua Silicon Carbide Micropowder Co., Ltd.

潍坊凯华碳化硅微粉有限公司

Company Overview

Weifang Kaihua Silicon Carbide Micropowder Co., Ltd., established in 2002, specializes in the R&D, production, and sales of ceramic-grade silicon carbide micropowder. Covering 40 mu (≈26,667 m²) with 19,000 m² of standard production facilities and a 2,000 m² office and R&D center, the company has an annual production capacity of 30,000 tons. Its micropowder features high purity, spherical particle morphology, excellent flowability, and strong stability, and is widely used in reaction bonding, pressureless sintering, novel silicon-carbon rod manufacturing, recrystallization sintering, honeycomb ceramics, railway braking systems, grinding and polishing, metal corrosion-resistant thermal spray coatings, nano-scale thermal insulation materials, additive manufacturing (e.g., binder jetting, SLM), and semiconductor wafer dicing.
