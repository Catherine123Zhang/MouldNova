Kongshi Tech Co., Ltd.

Company Overview

Kongshi Tech, headquartered in Dongguan—the heart of China’s ‘World Factory’—is an innovative service provider specializing in industrial applications of 3D printing technology. It develops proprietary industrial-grade stereolithography (SLA) 3D printers and operates an advanced materials lab holding over 20 patents. Its engineering-grade photopolymer resins achieve up to 75% of ABS mechanical strength, while high-temperature resins withstand heat deflection temperatures up to 280°C. Kongshi delivers mature, efficient, and scalable digital transformation solutions for global clients across diverse sectors.
