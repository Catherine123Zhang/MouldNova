Mianyang JiuFang Intelligent Equipment Technology Co., Ltd.

Company Overview

Mianyang JiuFang Intelligent Equipment Technology Co., Ltd., located in Jiangyou Hi-Tech Industrial Park, Sichuan Province, is a system integrator specializing in ultrafine powder engineering equipment. The company maintains a professional R&D team, pilot-scale industrial laboratories, and an advanced testing center. It holds over 40 patents, 10+ software copyrights, and certifications including the Weapon Equipment Quality Management System (GJB9001) and ISO 9001/14001/45001. Recognized as a National High-Tech Enterprise, a Sichuan Provincial 'Little Giant' SME, and a Sichuan Provincial Enterprise Technology Center, JiuFang’s flagship products—including hypersonic jet mills, high-precision air classifiers,分级-type impact mills, and particle-shaping machines—are widely deployed in powder metallurgy, cemented carbides, advanced ceramics, and lithium-ion battery cathode/anode material production.
