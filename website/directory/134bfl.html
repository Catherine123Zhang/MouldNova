Company Overview

eSUN, a brand of Shenzhen Esun Technology Co., Ltd., is dedicated to the R&D, manufacturing, and sales of 3D printing materials to advance the industry. The company masters production technologies for multiple thermoplastic materials including PLA, PCL, ABS, and PETG, meeting diverse performance requirements across applications. eSUN offers a comprehensive portfolio of high-performance, cost-effective, and broadly applicable 3D printing materials widely adopted in product design, industrial manufacturing, surgical medicine, cultural arts, education, and scientific research. As the primary drafting unit for China’s national standard on PLA 3D printing filaments, eSUN holds numerous domestic and international patents and operates production facilities in Xiaogan (Hubei Province) and Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam).
