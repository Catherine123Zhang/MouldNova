China Academy of Aviation Manufacturing Technology (CAAMT)

中国航空制造技术研究院

Company Overview

The China Academy of Aviation Manufacturing Technology (CAAMT), a direct business unit of the Aviation Industry Corporation of China (AVIC), comprises two research institutes and one listed company. CAAMT conducts foundational, applied, and engineering-transformation research in aviation materials, manufacturing processes, and specialized equipment—supplying advanced manufacturing technologies and process equipment to support the development of new aircraft, aero-engines, missiles, and technical upgrades of aviation factories. It also extends its advanced manufacturing expertise to other defense sectors—including aerospace, electronics, ordnance, and shipbuilding. CAAMT maintains long-term technical exchange and economic cooperation with research institutions and enterprises across more than 30 countries, including the U.S., Russia, Germany, France, Switzerland, Italy, and Japan.
