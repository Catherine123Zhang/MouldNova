Company Overview

Guangzhou Jingyun Electronics Technology Co., Ltd. (Jingyun 3D), established in 2015, is an integrated provider of modern intelligent manufacturing solutions—covering 3D printer R&D, manufacturing, sales, and holistic 3D printing system integration. The company holds multiple independent intellectual property rights and software copyrights, and serves as a council member of the Guangzhou Additive Manufacturing Industry Association, actively contributing to local 3D printing industry development. With comprehensive service guidance and extensive resource networks, Jingyun 3D ensures optimal, cutting-edge 3D printing solutions for clients. Its products are exported to the U.S., Russia, South Korea, Iran, Pakistan, and Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan—earning widespread trust and acclaim from global users.
