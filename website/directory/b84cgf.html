G-Share Intelligent Equipment (Anhui)

共享智能装备（安徽）

Company Overview

G-Share Intelligent Equipment is a national high-tech enterprise, a national SRDI 'Little Giant' enterprise, and a national green factory, with eight subsidiaries—including two overseas—and nearly 700 employees (26% R&D personnel). Its core businesses include binder jetting (for casting, metals, and ceramics) 3D printer R&D and manufacturing, turnkey 3D printing factory construction, 3D printed products and services, and precision sheet metal fabrication. It hosts dedicated centers for binder jetting AM engineering, machine tool auxiliary equipment, global marketing, equipment manufacturing, global operations & maintenance, and 3D printing services—aiming to become a world-class intelligent equipment manufacturer delivering top-tier products, quality, and service to accelerate China’s AM technology and equipment manufacturing into the global forefront and realize the 'Manufacturing Powerhouse' vision.
