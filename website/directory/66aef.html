Baoji Chuangxin Metal Materials Co., Ltd.

宝鸡市创信金属材料有限公司

Company Overview

Baoji Chuangxin Metal Materials Co., Ltd. (Chuangxin Metals), founded in 2005 with registered capital of RMB 20 million and occupying 50,000 m², is a key enterprise within the Baoji National New Materials High-Tech Industrial Base (‘China Titanium Valley’). Specializing in R&D, production, and sales of titanium and other non-ferrous metals, its products serve aerospace, marine, medical, chemical, optical electronics, vacuum coating, and titanium powder production for additive manufacturing—earning broad recognition from domestic and international professionals. Guided by the philosophy ‘Integrity drives development; details ensure excellence; service builds the brand,’ Chuangxin adheres to quality-first and customer-centric principles, embodying its corporate spirit: ‘Innovative development, integrity forever.’
