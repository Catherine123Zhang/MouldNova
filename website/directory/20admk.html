Company Overview

Wuhan Vismach Technology Ltd., a Sino-foreign joint venture established in 2005, delivers leading-edge solutions for sports health and medical applications through technological innovation. The company specializes in core technologies including 3D foot scanning and modeling, 2D image processing, pressure sensing, and intelligent recognition. Its flagship products—laser-based 3D foot scanners, CAD insole design software, and plantar pressure analysis systems—are all registered for software copyright and certified per relevant industry standards. As a pioneer in laser 3D foot scanning, Vismach’s solutions have undergone nearly two decades of market validation, earning trust from thousands of global users across the U.S., Canada, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Brazil, Japan, and Singapore.
