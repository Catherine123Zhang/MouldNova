Foshan Zhongcheng Smart Technology Co., Ltd.

佛山市众城智慧科技有限公司

Company Overview

Foshan Zhongcheng Smart Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in wax-based and various binder jetting (3DP) materials, as well as sand-based powder-bed 3D printing technologies. Among the few global companies capable of developing printheads (including Xaar printheads), waveform drivers, novel materials, and application-specific solutions, Zhongcheng has successfully launched proprietary lost-wax casting wax-model 3D printers and intelligent industrial sand 3D printers—widely deployed in jewelry, aerospace, automotive manufacturing, and precision casting. Its products are exported globally. As a ‘digital manufacturing engine’, Zhongcheng has delivered digital transformation solutions to over 100 enterprises worldwide—enabling breakthroughs in traditional manufacturing bottlenecks and delivering dual revolutions in efficiency and cost.
