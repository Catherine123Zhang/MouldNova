Huizhou Oryx Technology Co., Ltd.

惠州奥瑞科技有限公司

Company Overview

Since 2008, Huizhou Oryx Technology Co., Ltd. has served as the R&D and manufacturing center for PADT/Oryx Additive—Phoenix, Arizona-based OEMs with 30 years of additive manufacturing experience. Oryx develops and produces post-processing equipment for support material removal, including water-circulation agitation cleaners (SCA1200HT, SCA3600), large-capacity immersion tanks (SCA40K), and high-pressure waterjet systems (SCAWaterjet) for PolyJet support removal. It also offers the PCA series for photocurable resin residue cleaning. Oryx continues active R&D in ultrasonic cleaning, surface finishing (grinding, polishing, etching), coloring technologies, and solvent-free alternatives—including the proprietary 'Moshu Xi' cleaning diluent—and waste liquid/solid treatment systems. Oryx delivers efficient, integrated post-processing solutions that enhance manufacturing throughput, optimize process efficiency, reduce scrap rates, and increase print success rates.
