Company Overview

Norsbeary New Materials Technology (Suzhou) Co., Ltd. (brand: KEXCELLED) is a globally leading supplier of additive manufacturing materials, specializing in R&D, production, and sales of high-performance polymeric AM materials—including mature FDM thermoplastics and photopolymer resin product lines. Recognized as a Jiangsu Provincial Private Science & Technology Enterprise and National High-Tech Enterprise, Norsbeary collaborates closely with global leaders including LEHVOSS, Dow, Huntsman, and Covestro. It has filed over 40 patents, launched a European subsidiary in 2016, and established a Nantong branch in 2023 to scale production capacity. Guided by a customer-centric philosophy, Norsbeary delivers premium 3D printing materials and customized AM services to accelerate adoption of AM as a mainstream manufacturing paradigm.
