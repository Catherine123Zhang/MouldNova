Company Overview

Shenzhen Pengyang 3D Technology Co., Ltd., founded in 2009 and headquartered at the Shuibei International Jewelry Trading Center in Shenzhen, is a comprehensive 3D printing service provider for the jewelry industry. As an Industry 4.0 intelligent solutions partner, Pengyang offers integrated R&D, manufacturing, sales, and after-sales services. With a globally distributed engineering team covering major jewelry-producing countries and regions, its products are exported to over 40 countries, supporting jewelry manufacturers with competitive, high-quality digital manufacturing solutions.
