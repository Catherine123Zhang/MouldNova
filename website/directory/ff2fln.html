Ningbo Institute of Materials Technology and Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences

中国科学院宁波材料技术与工程研究所

Company Overview

The Ningbo Institute of Materials Technology and Engineering (NIMTE), Chinese Academy of Sciences—established in 2004 through joint construction by CAS, Zhejiang Province, and Ningbo Municipality—focuses on materials science, new energy, advanced manufacturing, and biomedical engineering. It operates a full-chain innovation system bridging fundamental research to industrialization. With over 960 staff—including 8 academicians and 7 National Distinguished Young Scholars—it has undertaken more than 7,300 research projects; its materials science discipline ranks within the global top 1‰, and it holds over 4,100 authorized patents. The Laser Extreme Manufacturing Research Center, founded in June 2022 under NIMTE, hosts a Zhejiang Provincial Key Laboratory and leads 20 national-level projects, with ~RMB 400 million in active R&D funding. The Center pioneers high-efficiency laser processing of hard-to-machine materials and structures, advances multi-material additive manufacturing, targets breakthroughs in kilowatt-class femtosecond laser processing, and aims to establish a transformative laser manufacturing demonstration line for hard-to-machine components—supporting major national strategic initiatives.
