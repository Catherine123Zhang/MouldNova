Company Overview

Xiamen Hanprint Co., Ltd. (Hanprint) is a national high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, manufacturing, sales, and service of printing equipment, with a vision to become a global leader in the printing industry. Founded in 2012 with registered capital of RMB 52.63 million, Hanprint operates production bases exceeding 50,000 m² and provides over 2,000 jobs annually. Its R&D team comprises more than 400 engineers. As of end-2024, Hanprint holds 785 authorized patents and serves customers in over 80 countries and regions. Recognized as a National 'Specialized, Precise, Unique, and Innovative' (SPUI) Little Giant Enterprise, a National Service-Oriented Manufacturing Demonstration Enterprise, and a National Champion in Manufacturing, Hanprint has also received prestigious awards including the German Red Dot Best Design Award and the China Patent Award. It is certified as a Fujian Green Factory in alignment with China’s carbon neutrality and green manufacturing initiatives.
