TrackScan Sharp Series Tracking-Based 3D Scanning System

TrackScan Sharp系列跟踪式三维扫描系统

Description

The TrackScan Sharp series is an all-in-one tracking-based 3D scanning system combining maximum speed, extended range, and ultra-wide coverage. It confidently handles measurements up to 8.5 m and achieves a high-precision volumetric measurement capacity of 135 m³—making it the premier solution for ultra-large-scale metrology projects. Standard equipment includes a 25-megapixel industrial camera, an embedded edge-computing module, full-battery operation, and true wireless data transmission—delivering precision performance across expansive workspaces.
