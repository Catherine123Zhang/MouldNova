CR-M600 Series Industrial Mold-Dedicated SLM System

工业模具专机——CR-M600系列

Description

The CR-M600 is the company’s second-generation industrial mold-dedicated SLM system, featuring an upgraded 8-laser configuration. Building upon the proven process stability and data consistency of the first-generation mold system, it dramatically accelerates production of industrial molds—including footwear and tire molds. Compatible with aluminum alloys, titanium alloys, nickel-based superalloys, cobalt-chrome alloys, stainless steels, and mold steels, it offers a build volume of 600 × 450 × 200 mm.
