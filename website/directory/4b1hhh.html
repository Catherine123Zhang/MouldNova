Shanghai Canal Materials Technology Co., Ltd.

上海运河材料科技有限公司

Company Overview

A globally recognized distributor of specialty chemicals and advanced materials, with over 20 years of experience serving plastic modification and metalworking industries—and extensive product integration for 3D printing applications. For FDM, it supplies toughening agents for PLA (e.g., Mitsubishi Chemical MCC), TPU resins (Huntsman), and silver-ion antimicrobial additives. For SLA, it offers core-shell silicone rubber additives. For metal powder processing, it provides specialized lubricants including ethylene bis-stearamide (EBS) and molybdenum disulfide. It also supplies a broad range of functional additives for diverse AM requirements.
