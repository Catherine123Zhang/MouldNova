Company Overview

Steinbach AG, Germany, is a specialist provider of 3D printing services for high-performance industrial ceramics. It supports both one-off custom parts and serial production, serving demanding applications across chemical processing, electronics, medical technology, aerospace, semiconductors, automotive, and mechanical engineering. Material portfolio includes alumina (Al₂O₃), zirconia (ZrO₂), silica-based ceramics (SiO₂), alumina-toughened zirconia (ATZ), and aluminum nitride (AlN)—all engineered to meet top-tier standards for precision, stability, and durability. As a one-stop solution provider, Steinbach AG manages the entire production chain: design consultation and material selection; additive manufacturing (ceramic 3D printing); post-processing heat treatment; and delivery of ready-to-use ceramic components.
