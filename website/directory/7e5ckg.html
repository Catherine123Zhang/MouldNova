Company Overview

Shanghai Meipai Industrial Co., Ltd. is China’s first specialized service provider for advanced ceramic 3D printing. Its offerings include ceramic component prototyping and new product development, batch manufacturing (printing services), turnkey laboratory/production line setup, proprietary ceramic printer R&D and sales, and development and supply of ceramic slurries, resins, and modifiers.
