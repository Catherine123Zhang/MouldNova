Yueqi Additive Manufacturing (Fuzhou) Co., Ltd.

跃启增材制造(抚州)有限公司

Company Overview

Yueqi Additive Manufacturing (Fuzhou) Co., Ltd., established in July 2025, is a technology company providing titanium alloy and polymer (PLA, PETG) 3D printing services. Its fleet includes large-format metal laser 3D printers and 20 polymer 3D printers, enabling metal part builds up to 800 mm × 800 mm × 1200 mm. Complementing its AM capabilities, Yueqi operates 25 CNC machining centers (3-axis and 5-axis) and high-precision metrology equipment—including coordinate measuring machines (CMMs). Its engineering team brings extensive experience in precision manufacturing, offering integrated print-and-machine turnkey services.
