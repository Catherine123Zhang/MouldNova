Foshan Guanglei Intelligent Manufacturing Co., Ltd.

佛山市光垒智能制造有限公司

Company Overview

Guanglei Intelligent Manufacturing, founded in 2017, is a high-tech enterprise providing end-to-end ceramic and multi-material composite 3D printing solutions. Recognized as a National High-Tech Enterprise, Guangdong Provincial Technology-based SME, and Guangdong Innovative SME, its core offerings include R&D, manufacturing, and sales of DLP-based ceramic 3D printers; ceramic/multi-material composite 3D printers; and dedicated 3D printing materials. Its self-developed Matrix DLP large-format multi-projector image fusion technology enables ultra-large-area ceramic printing—leading domestically. Its ceramic/multi-material composite 3D printer is the first of its kind in China, supporting true multi-material (including ceramics) printing.
