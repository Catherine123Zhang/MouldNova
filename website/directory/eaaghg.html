Company Overview

Luoyang E-Print Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. (operating as the Aisikai Luoyang 3D Printing Center) was established in October 2018 as a joint venture between Aisikai Technology—a listed company—and Weizhen Investment, with registered capital of RMB 35 million and a facility spanning 7,300 m². As an authorized direct sales and service provider for Aisikai equipment, it operates one of China’s largest-scale 3D sand-printing centers. The company specializes in 3D sand mold printing and castings manufacturing and services, offering rapid, professional solutions including mold design, casting process engineering, 3D sand mold simulation and validation, sand mold printing, and finished casting production. Its dedicated R&D team supports full-cycle service from part drawings through to final cast components.
