圣德科（上海）光通信有限公司

Company Overview

Santec (Shanghai) Optical Communications Co., Ltd. is the wholly owned Chinese subsidiary of Santec Corporation, Japan—a global leader in optical test and measurement instrumentation founded in 1979 in Komaki, Aichi Prefecture. Listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange since 2001, Santec maintains subsidiaries across North America, Canada, the UK, Australia, and China. With nearly 50 years of technological heritage, Santec holds deep expertise in MEMS, LCOS, OCT (optical coherence tomography), and optical thin-film technologies, consistently delivering customer-centric innovation and deploying these advanced optical platforms into broader industrial, medical, and research applications.
