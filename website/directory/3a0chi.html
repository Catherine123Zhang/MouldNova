Company Overview

Founded in 2013, Shanghai Zhuyue Environmental Technology Co., Ltd. specializes in powerful, application-specific dust, mist, solid particulate, and oil mist collection and filtration solutions for industrial manufacturing environments. Guided by a philosophy of delivering bespoke, high-performance solutions—not mass production—the company ensures every system is meticulously engineered to exceed unique customer expectations. Its engineered solutions enhance production efficiency and safeguard worker health and safety, transforming dust and mist collectors from simple cleaning tools into mission-critical productivity and occupational health assets.
